Website topic and title: Adventure Blog Title: the adventures of: Van Wilder

the adventures of: Van Wilder Is an adventure blog highlighting our retired ambulance van that is in process of being converted into a 4x4 off road camping machine.

Target audience

Adventure blogging has become very popular in the last few years. Even more specifically adventure van blogs. The target audience for this site would be people who enjoy traveling, adventure, van enthusiasts, hiking enthusiasts, as well as people who are looking for tips and tricks to making camping easier. People who are interested in 4x4 van conversions could be interested in this site as well. Also, people looking for ideas about cooking in the woods!

Site purpose

This website will give viewers a look inside a life of van life adventuring. The site will feature photography, tips and tricks for essential camping gear, camp cooking recipes that can be prepared at home ahead of a trip. In the future I would like to add how-to videos (provided by my husband) that would detail different aspects of completing a 4x4 conversion.
