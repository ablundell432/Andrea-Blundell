What is a domain name?

A domain name is a text based name associated with the numerical protocols of a website. Domain names are also used to indicate ownership or control of the website. It basically provides users with an easy to remember name to find your site.

Why are domain names so important?

On a basic level, domain names are important because without them, users would have to find or memorize IP addresses, which is a string of numbers associated with a site. Having a domain name also makes your business look more credible and could attract business. Having your own domain name can also make your business appear forward thinking and tech-savvy.

Why do domain names have to be unique?

Having a unique domain name is important because then no two companies can have the same domain, causing confusion for potential customers.
